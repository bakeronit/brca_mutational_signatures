## CHORD HRD prediction after FFPE impact correction

<img src="figures/chord_fig5-1.png" width="480" style="display: block; margin: auto;" />

**Figure 4: The HRD prediction scores before and after FFPE signature
correction**

The likelihood ratio test was performed to evaluate how strong if having
a germline BRCA1/2mutation increases the likelihood of having somatic
HRD based on number of HRD samples in each germline group with VUS
BRCA1/2 group and P/LP or VUS in other genes excluded.

<img src="figures/mosaic_plot-1.png" width="480" style="display: block; margin: auto;" />

if we include HRD subtypes:

<img src="figures/figure3b-1.png" width="480" style="display: block; margin: auto;" />

31 individuals had been excluded due to carrying P/LP in non-BRCA1/2
genes, out of these 29 individuals, 5 of them are HRD (BRCA1), 4 are HRD
(BRCA2) and 2 of them are HRD (Undetermined). 9 BRCA1/2 VUS cases were
identified as HRD, 7 in BRCA1 type and 2 in BRCA2 type.
